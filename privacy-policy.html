ShipMate Bot Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22.07.2024

1. Information We Collect

ShipMate Bot collects and processes the following information:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Product codes and their usage status
  • Server IDs where the bot is used

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To manage and validate product codes
  • To create temporary private channels for code redemption
  • To track the usage of product codes
  • To provide administrative functions such as listing and deleting product codes

3. Data Storage

The bot uses SQLite to store product codes and their status. This database is stored securely and is not accessible to unauthorized parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any collected data with third parties. All information is used solely for the operation of the ShipMate Bot.

5. User Rights

Users have the right to request the deletion of their data. To exercise this right, please contact the bot administrator.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the data we collect and process.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the bot's Discord server.

8. Contact Us
